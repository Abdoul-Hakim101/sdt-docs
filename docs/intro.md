### **SomNOG7: Technical Workshops & Conference - December 22nd – 26th, 2024**

The Somali Network Operators' Group (SomNOG) hosted **SomNOG7** in **Mogadishu, Somalia** from **December 22nd to 26th, 2024**. This event brought together ICT professionals, policymakers, and industry leaders to discuss Somalia’s digital future.

The event featured **3 days of technical workshops** and a **1-day conference**, focusing on the theme, "**Digital Public Infrastructure: Laying the Foundations for Somalia’s Digital Future**." Attendees participated in a variety of sessions addressing the country’s ICT challenges, growth opportunities, and digital transformation.

### **SomNOG7 Track 3: Software Development**

**SomNOG7 Track 3: Software Development** offered an immersive **Full-Stack Development with the MERN Stack** workshop over three days. This hands-on training was designed for software developers, computer science students, and recent graduates looking to advance their skills in building scalable, data-driven web applications. Participants gained practical experience in developing modern web applications, with a focus on the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React, Node.js), following a 60% practice to 40% theory ratio.
